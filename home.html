About

The main objective is that allows users to read and purchase a book online, add to the shopping cart and finally purchase
using transaction based on title, author and Category. The user can login using his account details or new customers can
set up an account very quickly. Using our Website the user can purchase a book online instead of going out to a book store
and wasting time. They should give the details of their name, contact number and shipping address. The user can also give
feedback. The books are divided into many categories like Biography, Novels, Non-Fiction etc.

With the rising popularity of portable devices onto which you can download, store, and read electronic versions of books,
e-books have been increasingly in demand. At this time, physical bookstores have no way of selling electronic versions of
books. But this type of product is a natural fit for an online bookstore, providing you with instant and inexpensive access
to a variety of reading materials.User can give feedback according to his opinion, books of his choice by category in feedback
form. Also, if user wants to read any book then he put the message in feedback form. One of the greatest benefits of an
online bookstore is the access these venues allow to reader recommendations and customer feedback.
